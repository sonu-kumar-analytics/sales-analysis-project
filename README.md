📊 Sales Performance Dashboard
📌 Objective

Analyze sales data to identify key insights for improving business performance.

🛠 Tools Used
. Power BI
. SQL
. Excel

📈 Key Insights
West region generates highest sales
Technology category is most profitable
Furniture category shows low profitability
Sales trend is increasing over time
Some cities have high sales but low profit

💡 Business Recommendations
Focus on high-performing regions
Improve cost efficiency in low-profit categories
Promote top-selling products
Optimize pricing/logistics in low-profit cities
